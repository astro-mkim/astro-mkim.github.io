I am an astronomer studying dust in comets and debris disks. In particular, my researches focus on looking at traces in the dust stemed from comet and around stars that reveal details about the formation and evolution of planetary systems. 

I am currently working on dust of comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko collected by Rosetta/MIDAS, in particular with respect to the morphology at the micro- to nanometre scale, to understand comets and dust growth in the early Solar System.

What am I working for as an astronomer?

I am currently studying the particles on Rosetta/MIDAS 
collection facets and visualizing the distribution on a map (ESA Rosetta/MIDAS; PI: Dr. Thurid Mannel). Furthermore, I am investigating if the particles can be categorized in different cometary dust populations. Finally, I am trying to determine the least altered particles and the properties with the highest potential to have preserved information about comets or the early solar system.
